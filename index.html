Our Projects

BACK TO SCHOOL EVENT

The "Back to School" event aims to provide underprivileged children with access to quality education through our donation drive, ensuring they have the necessary tools and resources to succeed. By contributing to this cause, we strive to bridge the gap in educational opportunities and create a more equitable future for all children. Your support will empower these young learners and help pave the way for a brighter, more inclusive tomorrow.

INTRODUCTION TO DISCPLIE AND LEADERSHIP SKILLS AT Ellerton Primary School(PREFECTS)

At Ellerton Primary School, the "Perfects" program teaches students discipline and leadership by encouraging responsibility and self-management. It provides opportunities for students to lead and support their peers. This fosters personal growth and builds essential life skills.
